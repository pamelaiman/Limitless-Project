CHARACTERS

Eddie Morra (played by Bradley Cooper)

  • Protagonist
  • Struggling Writer to Genius: Eddie starts as an unmotivated, unsuccessful writer whose life changes dramatically after taking NZT-48, a drug that enhances his cognitive abilities
  • Rapid Success and Danger: Under the influence of NZT, Eddie quickly rises to financial and social heights, but he also attracts dangerous attention and faces severe side effects.
  • Resourceful and Determined: Despite the risks, Eddie uses his newfound intelligence to navigate threats, solve complex problems, and strive for a stable future without the drug.

    • Vernon Gant (played by Johnny Whitworth)

  • Mysterious Benefactor: Vernon is Eddie’s former brother-in-law who introduces him to NZT-48, setting the entire plot in motion by providing Eddie with the drug.
  • Shady and Manipulative: Vernon has a dubious background and his motivations for giving Eddie the drug are unclear, suggesting he has his own interests in mind.
  • Tragic Downfall: Vernon’s involvement with NZT ultimately leads to his own demise, highlighting the dangerous and volatile nature of the drug.

    • Lindy (played by Abbie Cornish)

  • Supportive Yet Skeptical Girlfriend: Lindy is Eddie's girlfriend who initially supports him through his struggles but becomes wary of his sudden transformation after using NZT.
  • Courageous and Resourceful: When faced with the dangers stemming from Eddie’s involvement with NZT, Lindy proves her resourcefulness and bravery, even when directly threatened.
  • Moral Compass: Lindy serves as a grounding force for Eddie, challenging him to consider the moral and ethical implications of his actions and choices.
