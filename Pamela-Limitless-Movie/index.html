LIMITLESS (2011)

Directed by Neil Burger

"Limitless" is a thrilling science fiction film that follows Eddie Morra, a struggling writer whose life takes a dramatic turn when he discovers NZT-48, a revolutionary drug that unlocks the full potential of his brain. As Eddie's mental capabilities soar, he achieves unprecedented success, but he soon discovers that the drug comes with dangerous and deadly side effects. With his newfound intelligence, Eddie must navigate a treacherous path filled with powerful enemies and high stakes, all while trying to uncover the truth behind NZT-48 and secure his future.
